The Pizza Guys

of Carbondale

Best Pizza on both sides of the Lackawanna

Since we first opened our original location, in January 2016 on Church St. in Carbondale, we have continued to strive to provide excellent customer service and the highest quality food to the great people of Carbondale and the surrounding areas. Through this commitment to doing the best job we possible can for our patrons, we have worked to build a local base of wonderful and loyal customers.

Many of these customers have followed us to our new location at 46 N. Scott St., where we moved in August of 2018. At this new location, we will remain committed to providing our customers the highest level of service possible as we grow and become even more woven into the fabric of this fantastic community that we are so lucky to be a part of.

Our hard work and dedication led the Carbondale Chamber of Commerce to award us their Celebrating Progress Award on February 21, 2019.
